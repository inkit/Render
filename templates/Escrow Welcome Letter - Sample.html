Dear Elon Musk:

Thank you for selecting Inkit’s Escrow Management Services, an application that simplifies the way you can administer your client funds. With this service, you will receive a monthly consolidated escrow account statement as well as the standard monthly checking account statement. At each year end, Form 1099-INT interest reporting for your clients will be automatically generated for the account.

To assist you in initiating account maintenance, we have included the following forms and instructions:

To Open a Sub Account for Your Client Funds
  • Provide the specified information for your client using the Sub Account Opening Form along with a current IRS Form W-9 completed and signed by your client.
  • Fax the Sub Account Opening Form and the IRS Form W-9 to Escrow Management Services at 811-8328-3568.
  • You will receive notification of the assigned sub account number associated with each sub account.
To Transfer Funds
  • All deposits (including wire and branch teller) are received into your master account to fund an established sub account. To provide further credit to a sub account, please complete the Funds Transfer Request Form and fax it to us at 811-8328-3568. To notify us of an anticipated incoming wire, please call us at 811-8328-5369, option 4, in addition to faxing the form to us.
  • When disbursements are needed on behalf of a sub account, you may advance funds from the sub account to your master account by completing the Funds Transfer Request Form and faxing it to us at 811-8328-3568.
To Close a Sub Account
  • Complete the Sub Account Closing Form.
  • Fax the completed form to Escrow Management Service at 811-8328-3568

As always, we thank you for your business and look forward to servicing your escrow accounts. Should you have any questions, please contact Escrow Management Services at 811-8328-5369, option 4, Monday through Friday, between 8:30 AM and 5:00 PM ET. You may also contact your Relationship Manager or Cash Management Sales Representative.
